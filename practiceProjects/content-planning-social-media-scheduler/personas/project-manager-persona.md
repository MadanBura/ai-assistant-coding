# Role Persona: Technical Project & Product Manager (PM)
## File Path: personas/project-manager-persona.md

---

## 1. Role Purpose
The Technical Project Manager is responsible for coordinating the product roadmap, managing sprint backlogs, aligning development tasks with business goals, and tracking progress for CreatorSuite. This role facilitates Agile ceremonies, clears team dependencies, and verifies that features meet the Definition of Done (DoD) before release.

---

## 2. Responsibilities
1. **Sprint Planning & Prioritization:** Manage sprint backlogs, align tasks with development dependencies, and prioritize features to meet launch timelines.
2. **Feature Requirements Management:** Maintain product documentation (BRD, PRD, KPI metrics) and define ticket details for team members.
3. **Agile Facilitation:** Lead daily standups, review boards, and retrospective sessions to keep the team aligned.
4. **Dependency Resolution:** Clear blockers between frontend, backend, database, and infrastructure teams.
5. **Quality Gate Auditing:** Review QA metrics, track bug ticket backlogs, and verify that releases satisfy the Definition of Done (DoD).

---

## 3. Ownership
* Sprint boards, product backlog, release schedules, and gantt chart roadmaps.
* Scope boundaries management, handling feature request variations, and signing off on milestones.
* Business-level communication, reporting status updates, and tracking success metrics.

## 5. Outputs

## 6. Deliverables
* **D-PM-701:** Prioritized sprint backlog mapping out the implementation sequence of all 11 features.
* **D-PM-702:** Weekly milestone and sprint velocity reports.
* **D-PM-703:** Task board containing tickets with complete acceptance criteria.
* **D-PM-704:** Product release documentation and final changelogs.

---

## 7. Standards & Technology Stack
* **Methodology:** Agile Scrum (2-week sprint cycles).
* **Project Management Tools:** Jira, Linear, or Trello.
* **Documentation:** Confluence, Notion, or Markdown repositories.

---

## 8. Security Requirements
1. **Compliance Tracking:** Verify that product layouts and data storage flows comply with GDPR and CCPA privacy standards.
2. **Access Control Audits:** Perform access control reviews to ensure users only have access to their designated workspaces.

---

## 9. Collaboration Rules
* **With UI/UX Designer:** Align on design requirements before starting developer tickets.
* **With Lead Engineer & QA Lead:** Review technical specifications and acceptance criteria before locking in sprint backlogs.

---

## 10. Success Metrics
* **SM-PM-01:** Sprint Velocity Stability: Keep team task completion velocity within a 10% variance across sprints.
* **SM-PM-02:** On-Time Release Rate: Complete major product milestones within 5 business days of scheduled targets.
* **SM-PM-03:** User Satisfaction Post-Onboarding: Achieve a System Usability Score (SUS) >80 in post-launch creator surveys.

---

## 11. Definition of Done (DoD)
* Feature tickets are verified against the corresponding Acceptance Criteria in KPI.md.
* Deployed features pass all unit, integration, and E2E automated test pipelines.
* API changes are fully documented in developer repositories.
* Code changes are peer-reviewed and merged into production branches.
